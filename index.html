Discover Aden

Mon. May 25th 2020

Aden is a historic coastal city where nature meets culture. From the golden shores of Sira Beach to the ancient Aden Tanks, and from the towering Shamsan Mountain to the vibrant Crater markets — explore the landmarks and experiences that make Aden an unforgettable destination.

Tawila Tanks

Mon. May 25th 2020

Built over a thousand years ago, the Tawila Tanks of Aden are a remarkable engineering wonder carved into the rocky hills of Crater. Once used to store rainwater, today they stand as one of the city’s most iconic historical landmarks, attracting visitors with their unique architecture and cultural heritage.

Sira Castle

Mon. May 25th 2020

Overlooking the Gulf of Aden, Sira Castle is a centuries-old fortress that once protected the city from invaders. Standing proudly on Sira Island, the castle offers breathtaking panoramic views of the sea and Aden’s coastline, making it a must-visit landmark for history lovers and adventurers alike.

Aden Park

Mon. May 25th 2020

"In the heart of Aden lies Aden Park, a peaceful green space perfect for families and visitors. With its walking paths, shaded gardens, and playgrounds, the park offers a refreshing escape from the busy city — a place where nature, relaxation, and community come together
